We're Dallas Tech Labs — a small team crafting exceptional mobile experiences for iOS and Android. With 5+ apps launched, 100K+ downloads, and a 4.8 average rating, we build tools that solve real problems.

## Our Apps

### ISS Spotter
Track the International Space Station in real time with an interactive 3D globe. Get pass predictions for your location, see the current astronaut crew, and explore past and future orbits with the time simulator. Free on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6757094244) and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dallaslabs.issspotter).

### Quake Companion
Real-time earthquake monitoring powered by official USGS data. Live seismic data updated every few minutes, interactive maps, customizable alerts by magnitude and distance, and historical trends. Privacy-focused with no tracking. Free on [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dallaslabs.quakealert).

### Cabinet Doors Calculator
Professional engineering app for cabinet makers and woodworkers. Rail-and-stile door engineering, cabinet box design with multiple joinery options, sheet optimization achieving 75-85% material utilization, and professional PDF cut lists. Available on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6755406345) and [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.dallaslabs.cabinetdoors).

### Venus Companion
Your private wellness tracking companion. Daily activity and wellness pattern tracking with personalized insights, interactive charts, and data export. Privacy-first with local encryption — your data never leaves your device. No account required. Available on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6754580293).

### Polity Now
Independent, nonpartisan civic information platform. Personalized ballot lookup, interactive electoral maps for all 50 states with historical results from 1980-2024, legislative bill tracking, candidate comparison tools, and polling location finder. Free on [iOS](https://apps.apple.com/app/id6758637143).

## What We Stand For

- **Privacy first** — your data stays on your device
- **No tracking** — we don't collect or sell your information
- **Bilingual** — English and Spanish support across our apps
- **Craftsmanship** — we build tools we'd want to use ourselves
